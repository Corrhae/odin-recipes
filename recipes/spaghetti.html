Spaghetti


Description

Spaghetti is a recipe that originated in Europe almost a thousand years ago. It contains a tomato sauce and meat.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Garlic
  • Meatballs
  • Salt
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the Spaghetti
  2. Prepare the Sauce
  3. Combine Spaghetti and Sauce
  4. Serve
